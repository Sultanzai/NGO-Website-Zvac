ZVAC

“When we give cheerfully and accept gratefully, everyone is blessed.” Money is not the only commodity that is fun to give. We can give time, we can give our expertise, we can give our love, or simply give a smile. What does that cost? The point is, none of us can ever run out of something worthwhile to give

Our Goal is to help the underprivileged communities or underdeveloped societies to come out of the vicious cycle of poverty and be able to achieve what they deserve in the area of 21st centuries. and To implement various humanitarian projects in the fields of Health Care Services, education, vocational training, introduction of modern agricultural and Farming technology and conduction of research and the usage of its result for further improvements. and To work shoulder by shoulder with all communities to reduce continues suffering, provide better health and educational facilities, increase the chances of their work force marketability and eventually enhance their standard of living.
